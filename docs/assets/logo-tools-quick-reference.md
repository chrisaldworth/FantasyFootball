# Logo Generation Tools - Quick Reference

**Quick links and recommendations for generating team logos and icons**

---

## üèÜ Top Recommendations

### For Professional Team Logos

1. **Looka** ‚≠ê **TOP PICK**
   - **URL**: https://looka.com
   - **Best for**: AI-generated professional logos
   - **Export**: SVG, PNG (high-res)
   - **Cost**: Free trial, then $20-65 one-time
   - **Why**: Best balance of AI automation and customization

2. **Brandmark.io**
   - **URL**: https://brandmark.io
   - **Best for**: AI logo generation
   - **Export**: SVG, PNG
   - **Cost**: Free (watermarked), $25 one-time (full)
   - **Why**: More affordable alternative

---

### For Creative/Artistic Logos

3. **Midjourney**
   - **URL**: https://www.midjourney.com
   - **Best for**: Creative, artistic logos
   - **Export**: PNG (needs vectorization)
   - **Cost**: $10-60/month
   - **Why**: Highest quality creative results

4. **DALL-E 3**
   - **URL**: Via ChatGPT Plus or Bing
   - **Best for**: Quick logo concepts
   - **Export**: PNG (needs vectorization)
   - **Cost**: $20/month (ChatGPT Plus) or Free (Bing)
   - **Why**: Easy to use

---

### For Quick Templates

5. **Canva**
   - **URL**: https://canva.com
   - **Best for**: Quick logo/icon creation
   - **Export**: PNG, SVG (Pro plan)
   - **Cost**: Free (watermarked), $12.99/month (Pro)
   - **Why**: Easy templates, good icon library

6. **Kittl**
   - **URL**: https://kittl.com
   - **Best for**: AI-assisted design
   - **Export**: SVG, PNG
   - **Cost**: Free trial, then paid
   - **Why**: Good templates + AI

---

### For Vector Editing

7. **Inkscape** (Free)
   - **URL**: https://inkscape.org
   - **Best for**: Manual vector design
   - **Export**: SVG
   - **Cost**: Free
   - **Why**: Professional tool, completely free

8. **Boxy SVG** (Web)
   - **URL**: https://boxy-svg.com
   - **Best for**: Quick SVG editing
   - **Export**: SVG
   - **Cost**: Free (basic), $9.99 (Pro)
   - **Why**: Browser-based, no installation

---

### For Vectorization (PNG ‚Üí SVG)

9. **Vectorizer.io**
   - **URL**: https://vectorizer.io
   - **Best for**: High-quality vectorization
   - **Export**: SVG
   - **Cost**: Paid
   - **Why**: Best quality results

10. **AutoTracer**
    - **URL**: https://www.autotracer.org
    - **Best for**: Free vectorization
    - **Export**: SVG
    - **Cost**: Free
    - **Why**: Free option

---

## üéØ Quick Decision Guide

**I want professional AI-generated logos** ‚Üí Use **Looka** or **Brandmark.io**

**I want creative/artistic logos** ‚Üí Use **Midjourney** or **DALL-E 3**

**I want quick templates** ‚Üí Use **Canva** or **Kittl**

**I want to design manually** ‚Üí Use **Inkscape** (free) or **Boxy SVG**

**I need to convert PNG to SVG** ‚Üí Use **Vectorizer.io** (paid) or **AutoTracer** (free)
