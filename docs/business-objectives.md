# Business Objectives

## Vision
Create the most intuitive, data‑driven companion platform for Fantasy Premier League managers, offering AI‑powered insights, tools, and automation.

## Objectives
- Enhance users’ FPL decision‑making.
- Become a central hub for analytics, predictions, and trends.
- Build a scalable platform with continuous feature expansion.
- Grow a loyal FPL audience and convert a % to paid users.
- Monetise through subscriptions, affiliates, ads, and partnerships.

## Success Metrics
- 10k+ monthly active users by end of Season 1.
- 2–5% conversion to premium.
- <2s load time for live data tools.
- High retention: 60–70% weekly returning users.
