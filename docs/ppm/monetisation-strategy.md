# Monetisation Strategy

## 1. Freemium Subscription (Primary)
Free tier: basic stats, live rank, simple transfer tips.  
Premium tier: AI recommendations, deep analytics, projections, mini‑league tools.

## 2. Betting Affiliates
Contextual odds: goalscorer, clean sheet, match outcome.  
Commissions: £25–£50 CPA or revenue share.

## 3. Amazon Affiliate
Links to kits, books, laptops, accessories, training gear.

## 4. Ads for Free Tier
Google AdSense / Mediavine banners inside tools.

## 5. Sponsored Email Slots
Weekly FPL trends newsletter with sponsor placements.

## 6. Corporate Mini-League Packages
Businesses pay for branded dashboards + weekly insights.

## 7. Data/API Licensing (Future)
Provide expected‑points API to external apps.
